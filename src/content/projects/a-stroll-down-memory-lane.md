## The brief
Direct and produce a print campaign from concept through finished advertisement. I led as **Creative Director, Stylist, and Makeup Artist**, working with Leoj Chin on photography and Grace Bringham as the model.

## The concept
**"A stroll down memory lane."** The campaign's anchor idea: *every scent instantly evokes images, impressions, and positive emotions that echo the collective unconscious as well as our own personal history.* The fragrance world rewards storytelling, nostalgia, luxurious quietness, and a genderless point of view — those four were the brief I gave myself before any imagery was made.

## Trend research
I worked off three current threads in fragrance: fantasy-inspired scents, seasonal releases, and nature-inspired aromas. The concept lives where those three overlap — a nostalgic, sensory, slightly dreamlike memory.

## Mood boards
Five mood boards, one each for **posing, makeup, hair, styling, and photo aesthetic** — plus props and location. Every mood board had to read the same way: warm, soft, deliberate, gently unguarded.

## Production
The shoot was Wednesday, October 2nd, 3PM–7PM at 10 Whitaker St., Savannah. Hair and makeup 1:00–2:00, set 2:00–2:30, headshots 2:35–3:00, shoot 3:00–7:00. The call sheet, storyboard, and shot list are in the deliverables.

## The final campaign
The final flat advertisement leads with a single editorial portrait and the campaign tagline: **"What's your favorite memory?"** Mockups extend the campaign into print placement and digital surfaces.
